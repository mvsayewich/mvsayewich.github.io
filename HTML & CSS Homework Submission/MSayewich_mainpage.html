Summary

This website is dedicated to answering questions around what the weather is like as we get closer to the equator.

Python was used to assign 1,500 random Latitude and Longitudes, to find a listing of cities close to the equator. OpenWeatherMapAPI was then utilized to obtain weather information for these cities. All cities with relevant data were used to create a dataframe of weather information.

The data was analyzed across the three climate zones (Tropical Zone, Temperate Zone, and Polar Zone) and relationships were visualized through MatplotLib scatter plots to show:

  • Latitude vs. Temperature (F)
  • Latitude vs. Humidity (%)
  • Latitude vs. Cloudiness (%)
  • Latitude vs. Wind Speed (mph)
